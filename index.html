So Who Am I?

My name is Andres Mills Gallego and as you may have guessed correctly, I was not born in the US, I am from Colombia originally. I didn't study much post high school in terms of formal education, but I was (and still am) heavily involved in the construction industry. Working with the family business I learned the skills of my craft as well as developed extensive knowledge in the business side of things. Woodworking was always something that I enjoyed. I love being able to take a raw material and shape it into something new, beautiful and interesting! I have always strived to improve and to continue to learn as much as I can. I started my own woodworking business about 16 years ago and have been lucky enough to be able to shape it into a business that I am proud of. I am currently studying computer science and web development. I look forward to utilizing my current skillset to learn as much as I can in the months to come!
